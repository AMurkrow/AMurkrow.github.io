Pau Waelder is the Winner of the Banner-Strike Contest!!!
This contest was a collaboration between Velvet-Strike and the Banner-Art Collective. We asked for sprays and banners critically examining the U.S. War against Iraq. Pau Waelder will receive the game Counter-Strike: Condition Zero. Honorable Mentions went to KRN and Michael Szpakowski. Selected new submissions are included in the Sprays section.
